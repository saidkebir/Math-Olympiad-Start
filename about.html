Our Mission

AMO is the Algerian Mathematics Olympiad. We're a non-profit committee that started under the Ministry of Education. Our office is in Kouba, Algiers. We’re most active on Discord and post all updates there don’t forget to join.

What We Do

Find & Support Talent

Find and support students who love math.

Team Training

Train teams for national and international contests (like the IMO).

Fair Selection

Keep selection fair for everyone.

Organize Competitions

Organize national math competitions (ALMO).

Who Can Join

Our community welcomes diverse participants who share a passion for mathematics:

  • ■ Students, coaches, and staff members
  • ■ Guests (undergraduate/graduate students interested in AMO)
  • ■ Honorary members who have significantly contributed to AMO
Anyone supporting AMO's goals

How to Become a Member

Students

Algerian students who haven't taken the BAC yet and enjoy math and problem-solving.

Application: Register and take the online selection test (last one was in August)

Selection: Coaches choose based on test results and commitment.

Benefits: Access to training, resources, and competition opportunities.
